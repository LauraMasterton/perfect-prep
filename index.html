About us

What is Perfect Prep?

Perfect Prep is an online community of health concious individuals. This website is the result of our members recipes for quick and healthy meal prep ideas.

Do you meet up in person?

We don't currently organise any in person events but you can connect with other members and get together.

I have a slightly different version of a recipe, can I submit this?

Of course! We have a diverse membership and recipe variations are welcome.

I want to submit a recipe but I have an online presence, can you give me credit?

Absolutely, if you specifically want to be credited when you submit a recipe let our team know!

I have an idea for Perfect Prep, can I share this with you?

We welcome any suggestions to better our community. Get in touch and share your ideas!

Can I shoot my own photos to accompany a recipe of mine?

Of course! We really are a community and we'd love any photos of your creations.
