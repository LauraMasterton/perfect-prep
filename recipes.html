Broccoli Salad

  1. Roughly chop 1 head of broccoli and steam for 10minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, roughly chop 1 small red onion.
  3. Measure 30g pumpin seeds and 300g cranberries.
  4. When the broccoli is cool, mix all the ingredients together and drizzle with olive oil and season to taste.

Mango Smoothie

  1. Add 500g of mango, fresh or frozen to a blender.
  2. Add 30g of unflavoured pea protein.
  3. Add 300ml of almond milk.
  4. Add 20g of flax seeds
  5. Blend until smooth.

Summer Rolls

  1. Finely chop 1 cucumber, 1 small red onion and 1 red bell pepper.
  2. Drain 1 can of sweetcorn and 1 can of kidney beans.
  3. Place 50g of lettuce on a flour tortilla and season to taste.
  4. Place the other ingredients on the tortilla and roll the tortilla.

Spinach Soup

  1. Roughly chop 1 large potato, 2 garlic gloves and 1 onion.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add half a cup of white beans, potato, broth and mixed herbs. Bring to a boil and then simmer for 30minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat and pour in 200ml of coconut milk and toss in 300g of spinach.
  5. Blend for a creamy consistency.

Veggie Grain Bowl

  1. Cook 1 cup of your choice of quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Meanwhile, roughly chop 1 small red onion, 1 cucumber and 2 large tomatoes.
  4. Drain 1 can of chickpeas, drizzle in olive oil and roast for 15 minutes.
  5. Combine all of the ingredients into a bowl.

Vegan Burrito Bowl

  1. Cook 1 cup of white rice according to package instructions.
    2. Meanwhile, roughly chop 1 small onion and 2 cloves of garlic and saute.
  2. Roughly chop 1 red bell pepper and add to pan.
  3. Drain 1 can of black beans, 1 can of sweetcorn and empty into pan.
    4. After approximately 5 minutes place the cooked ingredients on top of the white rice and season to taste.

Raspberry Oatmeal

  1. Place 30g of oatmeal into a container.
  2. Pour in 300ml of almond milk.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of chia seeds.
  4. Mix the ingredients and slowly stir in 100g of raspberries.
  5. Place in the fridge overnight for a tasty breafast treat.

Butternut Squash Soup

  1. Roughly chop 500g of butternut squash, 2 cloves of garlic, 1 apple and 1 small onion.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add everything together and add mixed herbs. Continue to cook for 15 more minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in cocunut milk.
  5. Blend the soup until smooth.

Pasta & Spicy Marinara Sauce

  1. Cook 1 portion of pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Roughly chop 1 small onion and 2 cloves of garlic. Sauté for 5 minutes.
  3. Add 1 can of diced tomatoes to the pan and heat.
  4. Add a splash of balsamic vinegar, dried basil, dried oregano and chili flakes.
  5. Mix well and pour over the pasta.
